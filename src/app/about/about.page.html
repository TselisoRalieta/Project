Welcome to Safe Travel Lesotho

Safe Travel Lesotho is dedicated to enhancing travel safety in Lesotho’s highlands. Our app integrates official weather data and community reports to provide timely alerts, route suggestions, and emergency contacts. Whether you are a local or tourist, our goal is to help you travel with confidence and avoid hazards.

Stay informed and prepared wherever you go. Access offline maps, receive alerts about snow, floods, landslides, and blocked roads, and report hazards to help others stay safe.
